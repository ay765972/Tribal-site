About this course

Having confidence in oneself and one's environment is key to dealing with life's challenges and coming out as a winner. It is not only important to learn the methods to build confidence, but also the methods to maintain that confidence in oneself and others around, despite all odds. Public speaking is act of performing a speech before an audience, which could be formal, informal, or semi-formal depending on the kind of setting. Learning to speak well and putting forth valid arguments is a vital skill for the youth today.
